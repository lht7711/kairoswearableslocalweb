Analog-Digital Hybrid Smart Watch
Kairos created the world's first and only hybrid smart watch fully equipped with an automatic mechanical movement from Miyota / Soprod Swiss and a revolutionary transparent OLED display.
INNOVATION DRIVEN BY PASSION. KAIROS TEAM HAD TO DEFY CONVENTIONAL WATCH MAKING PROCEDURES AND PUSH THE BOUNDARIES OF ELECTRONIC ENGINEERING TO CREATE THE HYBRID SMART WATCH

Kairos wrote history by launching the world’s first ever true “analog-digital hybrid smartwatch” which included a full mechanical movement and an innovative transparent OLED display. It was a bold, almost “crazy” concept that only a start-up could even attempt. Powered by thousands of enthusiastic supporters from around the world, Kairos crowdfunded the project and eventually hit $2M USD in pre-orders within the first 6 months of launching. Despite several challenges, Kairos began shipping the products within 1 year of starting the campaign.

UPGRADABILITY
All Kairos Hybrid watches are built to be fully upgradeable. While most mass-produced CE products are put together by machines and glued together, Kairos watches were built by hand – one watch at a time by experienced watch makers before being transferred over to skilled electric technicians for further QC testing. Furthermore, the design allows for easy disassembly and assembly of components which allows for 100% upgradability of PCB, battery, and other components.
PATENTED DESIGN
Kairos Hybrid Smart Watches has obtained numerous utility (technology) patents as well as design patents including the internal case design as well as the C-shaped battery design which is necessary in order to house a mechanical movement in the center.
PRODUCTION IN PROGRESS
Since June 2015, Kairos has been producing, assembling and shipping Hybrid Smart watches to over 23 different countries. Starting in 2017, Kairos will be moving production to one of the world's largest watch making facility in order to ramp up its production for mass market.
