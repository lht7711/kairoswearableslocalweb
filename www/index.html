KAIROS WEARABLE LABS
Established in 2013, Kairos is a pioneer in luxury technology wearables that have evolved into providing leading edge health wearables and customized enterperise wearable solutions.
OEM TECHNOLOGY PROVIDER
Kairos provides a turn-key solution for traditional watch and fashion brands around the world in creating their own line of wearable products.
CUSTOM ENTERPRISE WEARABLES
Utilizing time-tested technologies from Kairos Wearable products, Kairos is able to provide customized wearable solutions for various enterprises that require additional level of security, safety and employee management.
HEALTH WEARABLES WITH BLOOD PRESSURE SENSOR
Kairos is currently developing a new line of health wearable products with a patented and medical grade accurate blood pressure sensor - a first in the world. Estimated release date will be summer of 2017.
