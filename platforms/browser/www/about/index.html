ABOUT US

Established in 2013, Kairos shot to fame in 2014 when it successfully crowdfunded over $2M in their maiden product - the Hybrid Smartwatch. Since then, Kairos continued to refine and fine tune its technology including the world's smallest smartwatch PCB and a robust, reliable proprietary firmware. By 2016, utilizing Kairos's time tested technology, over 30 brands have partnered with Kairos to launch their own line of wearables. Kairos also has recently been able to expand into the health wearable industry with a revolutionary blood pressure sensor as well as providing customized wearable solutions for enterprises.

WE LOVE WHAT WE DO
Challenges after challenges, Kairos team delivered results one after another. This is what drives us. This is what we do.
100% PROPRIETARY TECHNOLOGY
RELIABLE HARDWARE, STABLE FIRMWARE

As a start-up, Kairos was fearless. We did what no other traditional watch maker and electronic giants thought of doing. With the support of our investors and pre-order backers, Kairos also didn't have to worry about "ROI" on such a risky product such as the Hybrid Smartwatch with a transparent display and a full mechanical movement. Most companies in 2013/14 thought we were reckless. The concept itself was thought to be impossible to create.


By 2015, first shipment of the pre-orders began and history was made. During the ordeal, Kairos was put through countless amounts of challenges. We worked with numerous suppliers and even challenged component manufacturers to go beyond their comfort zone and challenge themselves in the process.


By 2016, Kairos possessed the world's smallest smartwatch PCB (T-BAND) and with the help of its original pre-order backers, had a reliable, time-tested and most importantly - a stable firmware.


This "platform" has enabled Kairos to take on further challenges such as creating a fully customized safety wearable band for a $62B Mining and Construction company and also start development of a revolutionary health wearable product with the world's first blood pressure (tactile BP) sensor.
