Hybrid Smartwatch Project WIW
WIW (Watch in a Watch) project brings together all the knowledge, experience and technology from building our original Hybrid smartwatch with uniquely different look. The top glass is made of sapphire crystal or hardened mineral glass (depending on the watch) and a capacitive touch sensor that provides 95% transparency. The display is placed on the same level as the watch dial plate which shares the real estate with a fully mechanical Quartz movement.
FUNCTION AND FORM. GET BOTH WITH WIW SMARTWATCH BY KAIROS WITHOUT ANY SACRIFICES

Just like Kairos’s original hybrid smartwatch, WIW project smartwatch offers best of analog and digital benefits in a watch.

OFF OR ON
Battery charging is now part of our life’s routine task, but we all forget to do it sometimes nonetheless. Kairos WIW smartwatch works like an analog watch – no need to replace the battery (for the watch movement) for 2-3 years. However, if you do end up charging up the onboard smartwatch battery, you will be able to enjoy looks of an analog watch with functions of a smartwatch.
MICRODISPLAY
Kairos is already working with some of the world’s most innovative component manufacturers to bring WIW project to life. The display for example is a 0.99 “ ultra lower power Memory-in-Pixel Reflective Type Color LCD that provides adequate resolution and brightness while delivering extended battery life.
