HYBRID SMART WATCH WITH TOLED
Kairos created the world's first and only hybrid smart watch fully equipped with an automatic mechanical movement from Miyota / Soprod Swiss and a revolutionary transparent OLED display.
HYBRID SMART WATCH WIW
WIW (Watch in a Watch) project brings together all the knowledge, experience and technology from building our original Hybrid smartwatch with uniquely different look. The top glass is made of sapphire crystal or hardened mineral glass (depending on the watch) and a capacitive touch sensor that provides 95% transparency. The display is placed on the same level as the watch dial plate which shares the real estate with a fully mechanical Quartz movement.
